CLUB

Our club is all about expanding our creativity with other individuals. We will prepare fun activities and all of the materials needed. With all of the collected items, we intend transforming them into nice decorations, pencil cases, pen holders, vases and more. Every student that will come will be provided with everything needed and at the end of the lunchbreak they may bring them home. We'll set relaxing music and you have permission to chat with your friends and make new ones. Experiment with new tools or even goof around and use your imagination. 10 minutes before the end of the break, we will clean our room and relax. We hope to see many creative, engaged and unique minds!

Every monday at 1:15 in the Spanish room
